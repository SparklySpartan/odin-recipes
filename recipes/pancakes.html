Pancakes

Description

Delicious, well known breakfast but also sometimes dinner. Put on butter, syrup, or peanutbutter, or jam, or ANYTHING, because you get to decide!

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • margarine or butter
  1. Put the egg, flour, milk, brown sugar, vegetable oil, baking powder and salt in a bowl and mix em up
  2. Get a pan over the stove and cook them with 1/4 cup of mix each till they look right
  3. Put whatever you want on them and enjoy
