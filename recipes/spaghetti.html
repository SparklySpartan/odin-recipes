Spaghetti

Description

So good and so easy. The best kind is mom's spaghetti, but sometimes I have to make my spaghetti, and that's what this is.

  • Sauce
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  1. Defrost the ground beef by keeping it in the fridge a couple hours before cooking time.
  2. Get some water to a pot and bring it to a boil on your stove.
  3. Put the meat in a pan and let it cook also on the stove.
  4. When the noodles are the way you want them, drain the pot.
  5. When the meet is cooked and broken up into a bunch of little pieces add the sauce and let it simmer a bit.
  6. Finally, when both components are ready, you add the sauce and meat to the pot with the noodles and mix them together
  7. I recommend adding parmesian cheese, and if you're a psycho like me add sriracha and/or tobasco sauce.
  8. The end!
