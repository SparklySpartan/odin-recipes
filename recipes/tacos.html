Description

They'll never be as good as authentic Mexican tacos, but these are pretty good.
They're always good, even not on Tuesday.

  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning / preferred spices
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  1. Defrost the ground beef and start cooking it on the stove.
  2. Once the meat is done cooking, drain the grease into a bowl.
  3. Add seasoning.
  4. Heat up the tortillas because cold tortillas suck.
  5. Set out the meat and plate of heated tortillas and all of the toppings you likw. For toppings I recommend
    • cheese
    • tomatoes
    • hot sauce
    • sour cream
    • guacamole (not for me though)
    • crushed tortilla chips (they add crunch you should try it)
    • beans, of course
